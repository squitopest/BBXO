Our Story

About Us

At BlossomBouquetsxo, every bouquet is a labor of love. What started as a passion for creating beautiful floral arrangements has grown into a boutique floral studio serving Long Island and surrounding areas.

We specialize in luxury custom bouquets for life's most memorable moments — proposals, anniversaries, birthdays, baby showers, and everything in between. Each arrangement is handcrafted with premium roses, stargazer lilies, and unique accents like designer wrapping, custom sashes, and butterfly details.

Every bouquet is made with love, care, and detail.
